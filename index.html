Featured Projects

Here are some of the projects I'm proud to have worked on :D

Stellar Cluster Membership and Classification with Machine Learning

Performed clustering on stellar data using various machine learning methods to determine stellar membership within a cluster. Further applied XGBoost to classify spectral and luminosity classes, successfully distinguishing stellar populations despite missing astrophysical parameters.

Determining the Sun's Motion from Solar Neighborhood Stars using Gaia DR3

Analysed Gaia DR3 data of F and G main-sequence stars in the solar neighborhood to calculate the Sun's motion relative to the Local Standard of Rest (LSR). Derived U, V, W velocity components using Python, performed Gaussian fitting to velocity distributions, and identified the Sun's apex and antapex in equatorial coordinates.
